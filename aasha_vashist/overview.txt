FENRIR PROJECT - ONLINE GAMING WEBSITE
========================================
PRODUCT REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT (PRD) - OVERVIEW

1. PRODUCT VISION
Fenrir is an online gaming platform designed to provide gamers with a unified
hub for discovering, playing, and discussing their favorite games. The platform
aims to foster a vibrant community where users can track achievements, compete
on leaderboards, and engage in real-time multiplayer sessions.

2. TARGET AUDIENCE
- Primary: Casual and mid-core gamers aged 16-35
- Secondary: Game developers seeking a platform to showcase indie titles
- Tertiary: Esports enthusiasts looking for tournament organization tools

3. CORE FEATURES
- User registration and profile management
- Game library with search and filtering capabilities
- Real-time multiplayer matchmaking
- Leaderboards and achievement tracking
- Community forums and discussion boards
- Live streaming integration
- In-game chat and messaging system

4. KEY OBJECTIVES
- Launch an MVP within 12 weeks supporting 10,000 concurrent users
- Achieve 50,000 registered users within the first 6 months
- Maintain 99.9% uptime during peak gaming hours
- Provide sub-100ms latency for real-time gameplay

5. MONETIZATION STRATEGY
- Freemium model with premium subscription tiers
- In-platform currency for cosmetic items
- Sponsored tournaments and advertising
- Revenue sharing with game developers

6. SUCCESS METRICS
- Daily Active Users (DAU) and Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Average session duration
- User retention rates (D1, D7, D30)
- Matchmaking success rate
- Community engagement metrics
